## ai emotion analyzer on Xilinx PYNQ-Z2 FPGA

music player project capable of playing chords, 4 different harmonics, play songs in fast-forward and reverse, display the previous, current and future notes in an OLED display, play on ADSR (Attack-Delay-Sustain-Release) mode, and analyze each song's emotion based on average pitch and tempo in between notes played.
